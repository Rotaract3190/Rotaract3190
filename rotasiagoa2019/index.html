WELCOME TO ROTASIA

A Multi District South Asian International Rotaract Conference

Rotasia is an international conference of Rotaractors and Rotaract Clubs in South Asia. It is an international gathering of Rotaractors in the region to exchange information, enhance fellowship, build networks and concentrate ideas and efforts to work together for a common agenda of youths in general. Rotasia is generally understood as a 3-4 day conference for youths with invitees from Rotary.
