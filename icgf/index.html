Speakers

This year's ICGF on Youth Service focuses on inspiring the next gen with most celebrated and inspiring personalities.

Tejasvi Surya

Member of Parliament

Tejasvi Surya is BJP’s youngest Member of Parliament in the Lok Sabha. He was elected from the constituency of Bengaluru South with an unprecedented margin of 3.31 lakh votes. He brings the perfect amalgamation of idealism and pragmatism to our country’s process of nation-building. He strives to be the voice of India’s youth, and the flag-bearer of Kannadiga pride. He upholds his core ideals strongly irrespective of the situation facing him, at the same time as relentless pursuing every objective that he has to accomplish.
