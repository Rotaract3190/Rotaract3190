about Anveshana

ANVESHANA - The DLTW (District Leadership Training Workshop) This is not just a Training, it is a Journey to discover true ROTARACTION.
At the DLTW, you will learn not only the responsibilities of each role but also how best to execute them.
It is also a great place to interact with your counterparts from other clubs and start interacting and share ideas, projects, joint projects, etc.
So Block Your Dates, Fasten your seat belts and Get ready to Discover Your Potential to lead your Club.
