About Rotafest

ROTAFEST is one of the oldest fests of Bengaluru which originally was started to provide a platform for the people who have graduated out of college and cannot attend college fests.

PRATHIBHA and event introduced by Rotaract Club of Presidency College in 2015, was adopted as a format for Rotafest during 2016. It is a platform for government school pupils to showcase their talent and creativity in various cultural events.

ROTAFEST 2K18 will feature PRATHIBHA on Day 1 and CULTURAL FEST for General Public on Day 2.

FAQ & RULES

Day 1

How many government schools can a Rotaract club get?

A Rotaract Club is allowed to get any number of government schools to participate.

What is the registration amount?

A Rotaract Club has to pay a registration amount of Rs. 500/- which includes one registration per event. The above-mentioned registration amount is a mandatory fee that a club has to pay irrespective of the number of events the club has participants for.

What if we have more than one participants for an event?

For any additional registration, a Rotaract Club has to pay Rs. 50/- (for solo events) and Rs. 100/- (for group events) per registration.

By when should the list of students and number of students are to be submitted?

The list of schools and the number of students participating has to be submitted by the Rotaract Club on or before 21st of October 2018.

Rules & Regulations!

The Rules & Regulations of the event are subject to change by the organising committee according to the various scenarios/shortcomings on the day of the event. The decision so taken by the organising committee is final.

Rules & Regulations!

The Rules & Regulations of the event are subject to change by the organising committee (if necessary) on the day of the event. The decision so taken by the organising committee is final.

Event Cancellation

The organising committee has the complete right to dissolve an event in case the minimum participation number isn’t met. In such case, the participation fee of the registrants will be refunded.

Media submissions to sound console.

On the day of the event, all the Karaoke, Music, Visuals, Photographs, etc., have to be submitted, at the sound console unless & until told otherwise.
